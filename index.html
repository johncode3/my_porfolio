About Me

I believe great design shouldn't just solve problems—it should look fire while doing it. My journey started in the world of pixels and professional printing, where I spent two years mastering the art of visual communication. Now, as a Y2 IT student, I’m bridging that creative background with the logic of full-stack development.
My typical day? Handling professional graphic design and web architecture from 8 to 5, then diving into university lectures from 6 to 9. It’s a high-energy grind, but it’s what allows me to build digital experiences that are as technically sound as they are aesthetically pleasing.
Whether I'm architecting custom WordPress listing sites, sharing university survival tips on TikTok, or documenting my coding journey, I’m focused on one thing: creating a positive impact through clean, thoughtful tech.
